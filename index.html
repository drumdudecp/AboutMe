My Personal Interests

I love music. I started on piano around 10 years old and then moved to the drumset around 11. I have bachelors and masters degrees in music and am a full-time musician and educator. I produce music using Ableton Live co-organizer of the Ableton User Group Salt Lake.

A few years ago I was introduced to a book called Getting Things Done by David Allen. It's all about personal organization and stress free productivity. Since then, I have been building a personal organizational system. I have read the book several times since and am very interested in the topic of productivity

My journey into personal finance started when I listened to Dave Ramsey's show. This got me into living frugally and getting out of debt. Since then I've begun studying investing and am working towards an early retirement path inspired by Jacob Lund Fisker's Early Retirement Extreme
